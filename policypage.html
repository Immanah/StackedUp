Our Return Policy

At Ozyde Boutique, we want you to be completely satisfied with your rental experience. Our return policy is designed to be fair, transparent, and customer-friendly while ensuring the quality and condition of our designer dresses for all our clients.

Important: As a rental service, our return policy differs from traditional retail. Please read this policy carefully to understand your responsibilities and our processes.

Easy Returns

Prepaid shipping label included with every rental for hassle-free returns

3-Day Rental Period

Standard rental includes delivery day, event day, and return day

Damage Protection

Minor damage coverage included with every rental

Quality Guarantee

All dresses professionally cleaned and inspected after each rental

✓ Standard Return Process

Our standard return process is designed to be simple and convenient:

1

Pack the Dress

Carefully place the dress back in the original garment bag and packaging provided

2

Attach Shipping Label

Use the prepaid shipping label included in your rental package

3

Drop Off

Drop the package at any authorized shipping location or schedule a pickup

4

Confirmation

Receive email confirmation once we've processed your return

Free Returns: All standard returns include a prepaid shipping label at no additional cost to you.

⏱ Return Timeframes

Our rental periods are designed to give you ample time to enjoy your dress while ensuring availability for other customers:

  • Standard Rental: 3-day period (includes delivery, event, and return days)
  • Extended Rental: Available for additional days at a pro-rated fee
  • Late Returns: Additional fees apply for returns received after the due date
  • Early Returns: We accept early returns but do not offer partial refunds
Late Return Fees: Returns received after the due date will incur a late fee of R150 per day until the dress is returned.

✨ Dress Condition Requirements

To ensure the quality of our collection for all customers, we require dresses to be returned in the same condition they were received:

  • Free of stains, tears, or damage
  • All original tags and packaging included
  • No alterations or modifications
  • No strong perfumes or odors
  • Returned in the original garment bag
Professional Cleaning: Please do not attempt to clean the dress yourself. All cleaning is professionally handled by our team after each rental.

🛡 Damage Protection & Fees

We understand that accidents happen. Our damage protection covers minor incidents, while significant damage may incur fees:

Covered (No Charge)

  • Small food/drink stains
  • Loose threads/buttons
  • Minor make-up transfer

Repair Fees Apply

  • Tears or rips
  • Major stains
  • Broken zippers
  • Missing accessories
Non-Returnable Situations: Dresses returned with severe damage, smoke smell, or evidence of dry cleaning attempts may be subject to replacement fees up to the retail value of the dress.

🔄 Cancellations & Modifications

We understand that plans change. Here's our policy on cancellations and modifications:

  • 7+ Days Before Rental: Full refund
  • 3-6 Days Before Rental: 50% refund
  • Less than 48 Hours: No refund, but rental credit may be issued
  • Date Changes: Free changes based on availability
  • Size Exchanges: Free if requested within 2 hours of delivery

Frequently Asked Questions

What if my dress doesn't fit? +
Contact us within 2 hours of delivery, and we'll expedite a replacement size if available. We recommend reviewing our detailed size guide before ordering and considering our virtual try-on options.
Can I extend my rental period? +
Yes! If the dress is available for additional days, you can extend your rental for a pro-rated fee. Contact our support team at least 24 hours before your scheduled return date to check availability.
What happens if I damage the dress? +
Minor damage is covered by our protection plan. For significant damage, we'll assess repair costs and contact you. Most repairs cost between R200-R800 depending on the extent of damage and dress value.
How do I return the dress? +
Use the prepaid shipping label included in your package. Place the dress in the original garment bag, seal the package, and drop it at any shipping location. We recommend getting a drop-off receipt for your records.
What if I need to cancel last minute? +
Cancellations within 48 hours of delivery typically don't qualify for refunds, but we may issue rental credit for future use. Contact our team immediately if you need to cancel, and we'll do our best to accommodate your situation.

Need Help With a Return?

Our customer support team is here to help with any questions about returns, damages, or special circumstances.
