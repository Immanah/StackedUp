Shipping & Returns Information

Everything you need to know about getting your rental delivered and what to do if you need to return or exchange an item.

Please note: This page outlines general policy. Refer to your specific **rental agreement** for binding terms regarding your order.

Delivery Information

1. Standard Delivery Times

We aim to deliver all rental items promptly to ensure you have them ready for your event.

  • **Processing Time:** 1-2 business days (for cleaning and quality check).
  • **Shipping Time:** 3-5 business days (depending on your location).
  • **Total Time:** Please allow **5-7 business days** for your dress to arrive after placing your order. We strongly recommend booking at least 10 days in advance of your event date.

2. Express Shipping

Need it faster? Express shipping is available for an additional charge during checkout. This service guarantees delivery within **2 business days** after processing.

3. Delivery Costs

Our delivery structure is simple and transparent:

Standard Delivery: R50.00

Express Delivery: R150.00

We offer free standard shipping on rentals over R1000.

4. Return Shipping

A pre-paid return shipping label is included in your delivery box. Simply use the same packaging to return the item to us on the last day of your rental period. Drop the package off at any designated drop-off point.

Returns & Exchanges Policy

1. Standard Return Procedure (End of Rental)

All rental items must be shipped back on the final day of your rental term, as indicated on your order confirmation. Late returns may incur daily late fees.

  • Place the item in the original packaging.
  • Affix the pre-paid return shipping label.
  • Drop it off at the courier's designated location before the end of the day.
  • **No cleaning is required** — we handle all dry cleaning.

2. Returns Due to Fit/Defect (Pre-Event)

If the dress doesn't fit correctly, or if you notice a major defect upon arrival that was not disclosed:

You must notify us within **24 hours** of delivery. The item must be unworn and all tags/seals must remain attached. We can offer:

  • A credit for the full rental price for a future rental.
  • An exchange for a different size or item (subject to availability and price difference).
  • A full refund only if a suitable replacement cannot be provided.

3. Cancellations

Cancellations 14+ days before rental date: Full refund or credit.

Cancellations 7-13 days before rental date: Full credit only.

Cancellations less than 7 days before rental date: No refund or credit will be issued.
