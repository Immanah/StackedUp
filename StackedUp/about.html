About Ozyde

We partner with trusted rental houses to bring luxury looks to your next event.

Our Story & Values

Our Story

Ozyde started as a small rental service with the idea that everyone deserves to feel glamorous, without the cost of full ownership. We collaborate with boutique rental houses and manage reservations, delivery and returns — leaving you to focus on the moment.

What we do

  • Curated luxury dresses & accessories for events
  • Professional cleaning & inspection between rentals
  • Delivery, pickup and flexible return options
  • Personal styling consultations for special occasions
  • Quality assurance on all designer pieces

Our values

Quality, reliability, and exceptional customer service. We partner with vetted rental houses so that the rental experience feels seamless. Our commitment to sustainability means we extend the life of beautiful garments while making luxury fashion accessible to more people.

Team

Our team of fashion enthusiasts and logistics experts work together to ensure every rental experience is flawless. From our stylists who help you find the perfect look to our operations team that handles cleaning and delivery, we're dedicated to making your special moments even more memorable.
