Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take? +

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, express shipping takes 2-3 business days, and same-day delivery is available in select areas for orders placed before 12 PM.

What is your return policy? +

We offer a 14-day return policy for unworn items with original tags attached. Return shipping is free for defective items, and for size exchanges we cover the shipping cost for your replacement item.

Do you ship internationally? +

Currently, we only ship within South Africa. We're working on expanding our international shipping options in the future.

How can I track my order? +

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number on our website or the courier's website to track your package in real-time.

What if my item arrives damaged? +

If your item arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damaged item and packaging. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund immediately.

Still have questions?

Our customer service team is here to help with any shipping or return inquiries.
